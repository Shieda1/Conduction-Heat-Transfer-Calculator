What is the conduction heat transfer?
Conduction heat transfer is the process by which heat is transferred fromo the hotter end to the colder and of an object. It is denoted by k.
How to calculate conduction heat transfer?
To calculate conduction heat transfer you should use the following formula:
q = -k * ΔT
where:
q = local heat flux density
-k = material conductivity
ΔT = temperature gradient
Why use conduction heat transfer?
conduction heat transfer is used in order to move the heat energy from place to place
